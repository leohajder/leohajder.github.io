About Me

My name is Leo Hajder. I am a programmer, musician and audio producer from Split, Croatia.

Whether in college or on my own, I have learned etc.

I am a volunteer at Srce, a local cerebral palsy association in Split, Croatia. Our goal is the social integration of our members, improving their quality of life, organizing their free time, and participating in various art projects. Some of my tasks are guitar playing, video and audio content editing, assisting in project organization, social media promotion, etc.

In my spare time I do music production and composing. I record, mix, produce and play guitar in local bands. I have also done a soundtrack for a documentary. I have participated in the Dean Rodney Singers project, hosted by a London based company Heart n Soul, which brings musicians and artists with and without disabilities together to collaborate on artistic content. Our work was on display at the 2012 Summer Paralympics in London, UK.
