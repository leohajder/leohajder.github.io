About me

My name is Leo Hajder. I am a 29 year old programmer, developer, musician and audio producer from Split, Croatia. I work as a junior backend developer at Web.Burza in Zagreb, Croatia.

I am good at object-oriented programming, web development, algorithm and data structure modeling and problem solving. I have learned Python (+ Django, Tkinter), C# (+ Windows Forms, ASP.Net, ASP.Net MVC), PHP (+ Symfony, Sylius), JavaScript (+ jQuery, AJAX), HTML (+ CSS, Bootstrap), SQL, Git, etc, and I'm constantly looking to learn and progress. I've also obtained the MTA Software Development Fundamentals and the Cambridge English: Advanced (CAE) certificates.

I am very enthusiastic, eager to learn, open minded, friendly, outgoing and adaptive. I can switch between working in a team and on my own, while trying to be as professional and as thorough as I can be with any given task.

I volunteer at the local Cerebral Palsy association Srce in Split, Croatia. Our major goal is the social integration of people who suffer from cerebral palsy and authism, as well as improving their quality of life, organizing their free time, and participating in other activities such as concerts, musicals and other art projects. Some of the tasks I do are guitar playing, video and audio content editing, assisting in project organization, social media promotion, etc.

In my spare time I do music production and composing. I record, mix, produce and play guitar in local bands. I have also done a soundtrack for a documentary. All with an alternative approach, using mostly free software.
