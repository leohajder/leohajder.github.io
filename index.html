Bio

My name is Leo Hajder. I am a 29 year old programmer, developer, musician and audio producer from Split, Croatia. I work as a junior backend developer at web.burza in Zagreb, Croatia. My main area of work is object-oriented programming in web application development.

I know Python + Django, Tkinter, C# + Windows Forms, ASP.Net MVC, PHP + Symfony, Sylius, JavaScript + jQuery, AJAX, Handlebars.js, HTML + CSS, Bootstrap, SQL + MySQL, Microsoft SQL Server, SQLite, Git, etc.

I volunteer at the Cerebral Palsy association Srce in Split, Croatia. Our goal is the social integration of our members, improving their quality of life, organizing their free time, and participating in various art projects. Some of my tasks are guitar playing, video and audio content editing, assisting in project organization, social media promotion, etc.

In my spare time I do music production and composing. I record, mix, produce and play guitar in local bands. I have also done a soundtrack for a documentary. I have participated in the Dean Rodney Singers project, hosted by a London based company Heart N Soul, which brings musicians and artists with and without disabilities together to collaborate on creating artistic content. Our work was on display at the 2012 Summer Paralympics in London, UK.
